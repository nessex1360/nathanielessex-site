Who is Nathan Essex?

MBA, fintech strategist, author and inventor.

Raised by Caribbean Catholic military engineers, Nathan was the artist in the family — the kid who came home with novels under his arm while everyone else was building bridges. He kept the artist's instincts and added the engineer's discipline.

Ten-plus years in corporate finance followed: inside major international logistics companies and on the founding side of startups that grew into full enterprises. He has built businesses from scratch and helped others scale theirs, working in the rooms where strategy and operations actually meet.

He is, by his own admission, a Renaissance man — orchestral musician, family man, novelist, builder. His core strength is far transfer: taking a pattern from one domain and applying it cleanly in another, across disciplines that rarely speak to each other.

What ties it together is service. The work is the work, and the point of the work is to be a useful citizen.

— Nathan
