Nathan Essex is a fintech strategist, author, and inventor with more than a decade of experience in corporate finance. Raised by Caribbean Catholic military engineers, he grew up at the intersection of discipline, creativity, and technical rigor — and emerged as the artist in the family. His work reflects a talent for crossing domains and applying broad knowledge to novel, complex problems.

Across his career, Nathan has helped startups grow into full enterprises, built businesses from scratch, and contributed inside one of the world’s largest international logistics companies. An orchestral musician and family man, he brings a Renaissance mindset to everything he does, combining strategic thinking with creative invention.

Known for his breadth of knowledge and force of personality, Nathan is focused on creating useful things that endure. Whether in business, writing, or invention, he is driven by service and by the goal of being a useful citizen.
